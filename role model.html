Fusajiro Yamauchi is the orignal founder of the company now known as Nintendo. Yamauchi lived in kyoto,japan with his wife and daughter. Yamauchi was born november 22,1859 and passed away january 1,1940.

Why Fusajiro Yamauchi is my role model

I picked Yamauchi because I really enjoyed growing up with nintendo games and systems, and always wanted to share how I felt as a child playing games like mario,legend of zelda,on systems like game cube, nintendo 64, wii, and dsi, by creating iconic /revolutionary games for future genarations to enjoy.
